MAFW Spring Picnic for Members and Friends at the Duke Island Park Oak Pavilion (off to the right in the park).  If you have never been there, it's a beautiful venue along the South Branch of the Raritan with great rides. It has a giant gazebo, so rain is not a factor.     

Come for the rides before the picnic (riding at 9:30)--or just for the BBQ (~12 noon). Bring a friend, spouse or significant other. No $$ charge! Check out the 7-day ride schedule for rides of all paces. Admission: food to share with approximately 6 others. If your last name begins with the following letter, please bring:A-H Salad     I-P  Snacks, Appetizers     Q-Z Desserts 

Hotdogs, burgers, wings, drinks, and fixins will be provided.  NO BYO. The park does not allow alcoholic beverages.

As always - please contact Mary Conrad (ammcc5@aol.com) if you can volunteer to help out that day, especially before to set up.  THANK YOU in advance to our volunteers' continued support as we would not have a club without you!!!
