If you are a club member interested in becoming a Ride leader, please read the Ride Leader Handbook. If you are comfortable with the role and responsibilities described in the Handbook, email your request to become a ride leader to the Ride Coordinator at ridecoordinator@mafw.org.

You will be asked to participate in two rides with a ride leader mentor. These rides will provide you an opportunity to observe and talk with a ride leader that the club feels is exemplary. Mark Jay, recorder.dulcian@gmail.com is the New Ride Leader Mentorship Coordinator. He will help you find a mentor and answer any questions you might have about being a ride leader.

When you have completed the two mentorship rides, inform the ride coordinator and you will be added to the list of active ride leaders.
