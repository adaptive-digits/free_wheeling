MAFW Bicycle Adventures 2017

The Club designates "as official" a number of cycling trips or one-day events each year. These trips are coordinated by Club captains, but all registrations and travel arrangements are up to each member. These events and trips are open to all members of the Club.

The Club also organizes and sponsors its own event rides and trips such as the Lehigh Valley Trail Ride, the Lake George Tour, the New Member Ride & Lunch and others that are restricted to club members only (residents of the immediate household of members are the only exception). Non-members of the club may participate in club trips by paying an additional $24 fee to join the club for a one year membership.

Watch this space for events in 2017 which MAFW members may be interested in attending. If a MAFW member has agreed to be the "event coordinator" their contact information is listed. If you are attending a Special Event or Multi-Day tour, please notify Liz Jennison: drlizjen@gmail.com and it will be added to this list. You do not have to be the MAFW leader on the event to have it listed here.

If you'd like to organize a tour, contact Liz Jennison, our Adventures Coordinator: Liz Jennison: drlizjen@gmail.com

Bike MS: City to Shore (September 23-24, 2017)

MAFW Coordinator: Michael Chenkin (mchenkin@gmail.com)

http://main.nationalmssociety.org/site/TR/Bike/PAEBikeEvents?pg=entry&fr_id=28216

Your participation will drive MS research forward faster, and deliver services to those who face the challenges of MS every day so they can live their best lives.

This 2-day event, with route options for all cycling abilities from 25-180 miles, will take you through the flat terrain and back roads of South Jersey to the blueberry fields of Hammonton. At the end of the ride, you and 7,000 cyclists will roll into family-friendly Ocean City to cheering volunteers, family, and an atmosphere that couldn't be more electric.
