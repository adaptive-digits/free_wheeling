Saturday, April 21st we will kick off the 2018 Women on Wheels season! Rides will leave out of Pluckemin at 9:30 am. Female riders of all levels are welcome: riding groups will be decided the morning of based on the group that shows up to ride. WOW rides will be planned once a month for the 2018 season, so be on the lookout for dates of future rides and locations soon!

Let's face it, we all know that men and women are different, and this certainly translates into different cycling styles and experiences. Women riding with women can create a special dynamic and bond between those participating, helping to build their riding confidence and skills. The MAFW would like to promote the advantages of women riding with women to female riders looking for a different experience.
