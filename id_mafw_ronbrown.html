Ron Brown

Friday, Feb. 23rd we lost a dear friend and an incredible contributor to our cycling community.  In the past 24 hours, I have seen and heard many kind words Ron Brown's friends have publicly and privately shared, while I've struggled to put words on paper. He "lived his life to the fullest," was "one of the nicest guys I've ever known," was a "kind, sweet man whose life was taken way too early," was "one of the most courageous people I've known",,, I don't think we can say enough about Ron as a person or friend.  

 

We send our sincerest condolences to Tracy and his and her families through this very difficult time. Let's please continue to share words, pictures and memories so that we can honor him and provide comfort to all of his family and friends.  He will be missed by all.  

 

 

Sincerely,
