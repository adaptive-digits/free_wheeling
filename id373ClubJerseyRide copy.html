MAFW International Jersey Ride!

RESCHEDULED TO: August 25th. Rides start @ Plukemin Bed One

Contest is at 9:00am

Rides Commence at 9:30am

   

 The theme of this year's ride will be INTERNATIONAL JERSEYS. All riders are encouraged to wear jerseys that display INTERNATIONAL DESTINATIONS. The ride is open to all regardless of what you wear.    

 

Prizes will be awarded to those riders whose jerseys display the furthest distance from Pluckemin.

Each rider needs to determine, in kilometers, the distance from Pluckemin to their international starting point and bring that information to the ride.

Only those riders who actually did the rides displayed on their jersey will be eligible to win gift certificates. We're using the honor system here.

Gift certificates of $35, $25 and $15 will be awarded to those who traveled the furthest..

All rides will be from Pluckemin at all paces. arnie@teledge-usa.com
